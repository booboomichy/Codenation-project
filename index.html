The Caribbean is a group of islands in the Caribbean Sea to the east of North America, simply known to many as paradise! Jamaican culture is unique and vibrant and is famous across the world. From Jamaican music, dancehall and food to its arts and crafts all derived from its rich heritage.
The first things that come to mind when thinking about Jamaica is the music of Bob Marley, world famous Jerk chicken and the fastest man on the planet Usain Bolt.
